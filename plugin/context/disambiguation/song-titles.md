## Key Songwriting Teams

- **Jerry Garcia & Robert Hunter** - The primary songwriting partnership, responsible for most classic Dead songs
- **Bob Weir & John Perry Barlow** - Secondary partnership, responsible for many beloved songs
- **Bob Weir & Robert Hunter** - Occasional collaboration (e.g., "Sugar Magnolia")
- **Phil Lesh & Robert Hunter** - Rare collaboration (e.g., "Box of Rain")
- **Mickey Hart & Robert Hunter** - Occasional collaboration (e.g., "Fire on the Mountain")

## Additional Context Clues

When disambiguating, consider:
1. **Album names** - Grateful Dead albums like American Beauty, Workingman's Dead, Europe '72
2. **Time period** - Most classic GD songs are from 1969-1974
3. **Live performance context** - Grateful Dead were known for live performances and improvisation
4. **Musical style** - Psychedelic rock, country-rock, folk, blues, jazz fusion
5. **Lyricist mention** - Robert Hunter or John Perry Barlow are key indicators
