## About The Bahr Gallery

The Bahr Gallery is dedicated to finding good homes for stunning psychedelic posters of the late 1960s. Located in the charming Long Island village of Oyster Bay, the gallery specializes in first printings, often signed, of Avalon, Fillmore, and other iconic venue posters which are highly prized by collectors and connoisseurs of the period.

## Collection Details

### Size and Scope
- **Total Collection**: More than 300 framed pieces
- **On Display**: 60+ pieces at any given time
- **New Additions**: 5-10 pieces framed and added each month from the owner's substantial collection
- **Scope**: Larger than many museum exhibits

### Specialization
- Psychedelic rock posters from the late 1960s and 1970s
- First printings of iconic venue posters
- Avalon Ballroom posters
- Fillmore posters (Fillmore West, Fillmore East)
- Often signed by artists
- Previously authenticated pieces bought at auction from reliable, reputable vendors

### Quality Standards
- Each work is carefully matted and framed
- Museum-quality glass
- Stylistic framework that enhances the art while letting it stand out
- Professional authentication from reputable auction vendors

## Museum Credentials

The Bahr Gallery has established museum-level credibility:
- Pieces have hung in the **Nassau County Museum of Art**
- 11 pieces recently returned from a loan to the **New-York Historical Society Museum** in New York City
- Gallery includes educational placards describing the times and contextual background of the artworks
