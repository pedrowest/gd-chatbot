# A Comprehensive Guide to Grateful Dead Online Resources

This guide provides a curated list of websites and online resources for fans of the Grateful Dead. The resources are categorized for easy navigation, covering everything from official band sites to fan communities and academic archives.

## I. Official Grateful Dead Resources

This section includes the official websites for the Grateful Dead, its members, and related projects.

| Category | Website | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Official Band Site** | [Grateful Dead](https://www.dead.net/) | The central hub for all things Grateful Dead, including news, music, and community forums. |
| **Official Merchandise** | [Grateful Dead Shop](https://www.gratefuldeadshop.com/) | The official online store for Grateful Dead merchandise. |
| **Spin-off Band** | [Dead & Company](https://deadandcompany.com/) | The official website for the band Dead & Company, featuring tour dates and news. |
| **Jerry Garcia** | [Jerry Garcia](https://jerrygarcia.com/) | The official website of Jerry Garcia, with information about his life and music. |
| **Bob Weir** | [Bob Weir](https://bobweir.net/) | The official website of Bob Weir, featuring news, tour dates, and music. |
| **Phil Lesh** | [Phil Lesh](https://www.phillesh.net/) | The official website of Phil Lesh. |
| **Mickey Hart** | [Mickey Hart](https://mickeyhart.net/) | The official website of Mickey Hart, showcasing his various projects. |
| **Bill Kreutzmann** | [Bill Kreutzmann](https://www.billkreutzmann.com/) | The official website of Bill Kreutzmann. |

## II. Archives and Live Recordings

This section provides links to the most significant archives of Grateful Dead live recordings.

| Website | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/GratefulDead) | The most comprehensive collection of live Grateful Dead recordings, with thousands of shows available for streaming and download. |
| [Grateful Dead Archive Online (GDAO)](https://www.gdao.org/) | A project of the UC Santa Cruz Library, providing access to a vast collection of digitized materials from the Grateful Dead Archive. |
| [Relisten.net](https://relisten.net/grateful-dead) | A user-friendly platform for streaming a vast collection of Grateful Dead and other live music recordings. |
| [Nugs.net](https://www.nugs.net/grateful-dead-concerts-live-downloads-in-mp3-flac-or-online-music-streaming/) | Offers high-quality soundboard recordings of Grateful Dead concerts for purchase and streaming. |

## III. Setlist Databases

These websites are dedicated to documenting the setlists of every Grateful Dead show.

| Website | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| [JerryBase](https://jerrybase.com/) | A comprehensive and professionally maintained database of Grateful Dead and Jerry Garcia setlists. |
| [The SetList Program](https://www.setlists.net/) | A searchable database of Grateful Dead setlists from 1965 to 1995, with user comments. |
| [GDSets.com](https://gdsets.com/) | Features setlists, ticket stubs, concert posters, and more. |
| [Grateful Stats](https://gratefulstats.com/) | A database of Grateful Dead shows, tunes, and venues with statistical analysis. |

## IV. Fan Communities and Forums

Connect with other Deadheads, share stories, and discuss the music.

| Platform | Link | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Reddit** | [r/gratefuldead](https://www.reddit.com/r/gratefuldead/) | A large and active community of Deadheads on Reddit. |
| **Official Forums** | [Dead.net Forums](https://www.dead.net/forum) | The official forums on the Grateful Dead website. |
| **RuKind.com** | [RuKind Forums](https://www.rukind.com/viewforum.php?f=311) | A forum for discussing the music of the Grateful Dead. |

## V. Media and Further Exploration

This section includes links to podcasts, documentaries, books, and other media related to the Grateful Dead.

| Category | Resource | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Official Podcast** | [The Good Ol' Grateful Deadcast](https://www.dead.net/deadcast) | The official podcast of the Grateful Dead, exploring the music and mythology of the band. |
