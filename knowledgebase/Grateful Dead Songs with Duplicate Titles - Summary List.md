## Most Likely to Cause Confusion

These songs have the highest risk of confusion due to other famous versions:

### High Confusion Risk:
1. **Fire on the Mountain** - Marshall Tucker Band version (1975) came FIRST
2. **Loser** - Beck's 1993 hit is very famous
3. **Eyes of the World** - Fleetwood Mac and Rainbow both had songs with this title
4. **Friend of the Devil** - Covered by Bob Dylan, Tom Petty, Mumford & Sons
5. **Comes A Time** - Neil Young's 1978 song is well-known
6. **Dark Star** - Crosby, Stills & Nash had a different song with this title
7. **Scarlet Begonias** - Sublime's cover is popular among younger audiences
8. **Candyman** - Christina Aguilera's 2007 pop hit is very different

### Moderate Confusion Risk:
- Casey Jones (traditional folk song, many versions)
- Bird Song (multiple artists)
- Brown Eyed Women (covered by many)
- Sugaree (many versions, including pre-GD songs)
